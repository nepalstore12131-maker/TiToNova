How to Make Videos Without a Camera (Using AI)

Creating videos used to require a camera, editing skills, and a lot of time. Now, with AI tools, you can create videos without filming anything.

Step 1: Write Your Script

Use ChatGPT to generate a script for your video. Keep it simple and clear.

Step 2: Use AI Video Tools

Tools like Pictory or InVideo AI can turn your script into a full video automatically.

Step 3: Add Voice

Use ElevenLabs to create realistic voiceovers without recording your voice.

Step 4: Export and Share

Download your video and upload it to YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram.

Final Thoughts

You don’t need a camera to start creating content anymore. AI tools make it fast and easy for anyone.
