How to Make Videos Without a Camera (Using AI)

You don’t need a camera, expensive gear, or editing skills to create videos anymore. With AI tools, you can turn simple ideas into full videos in minutes.

What you’ll learn:
• Create videos without filming
• Use AI tools step-by-step
• Publish content on YouTube or TikTok

Step 1: Write Your Script

Start by writing a simple script. You can use ChatGPT to generate ideas or full scripts.

💡 Example prompt: “Write a 60-second YouTube script about making money online”

Step 2: Turn Script into Video

Use AI video tools like:

  • Pictory
  • InVideo AI

These tools convert your text into video automatically with visuals and subtitles.

Step 3: Add AI Voice

Use tools like ElevenLabs to generate realistic voiceovers without recording your own voice.

Step 4: Export and Upload

Download your video and upload it to platforms like YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram.

Final Thoughts

AI makes video creation fast and accessible. You can start today with zero experience.
